✦ FAQ

Got Questions?

What file formats does MIMO support?
MIMO supports PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, and PNG files. PDFs are recommended for the best print quality. You can convert most documents to PDF before uploading.
How long is my print code valid?
Your 4-digit print code is valid for 24 hours after payment. If you don't collect your prints within this window, your payment is automatically refunded.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major UPI apps (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit/credit cards, and net banking. All payments are processed securely via Cashfree.
Is my document data private and safe?
Absolutely. Your documents are encrypted in transit, and permanently deleted from our servers immediately after printing. We never store, read, or share your files.
