Our Mission

MIMO is a smart self-service printing kiosk network designed to make printing easy, fast, cashless, and secure for modern campus students. Created by VisionPrintt Technologies, our platform solves the daily frustrations of long lines, exact change requirements, and lack of document privacy at campus print shops.

With MIMO, you simply upload your documents from your phone, customise your print settings, pay securely via UPI or cards, and collect your physical prints from the nearest campus kiosk using a secure 4-digit code — all in under 60 seconds.

We believe that printing should be as seamless as sending a WhatsApp message. Students shouldn't have to carry coins, wait in lines, or worry about strangers handling their documents. Our kiosk infrastructure handles everything securely in the cloud, so your files never touch a shared computer.

Currently deployed across REVA University campus, MIMO kiosks support both black & white laser printing and full-colour inkjet printing. We support PDF, Word documents, PowerPoint presentations, Excel sheets, and image files including HEIC from iPhones.

How MIMO Works

The MIMO printing experience is built around three simple steps that any student can complete from their smartphone in under a minute:

  • Upload: Open printmimo.tech in any mobile browser. Select your file — PDF, Word, image, or presentation — directly from your phone's storage, iCloud, or Google Drive.
  • Customise & Pay: Choose your print settings (colour mode, copies, duplex, page range). Review the live price preview, then pay securely via UPI, debit, or credit card through our Cashfree-powered gateway.
  • Collect: Walk to the nearest MIMO kiosk on campus. Enter your 4-digit secure print code on the touch screen. Your printout is ejected in seconds.

There are no apps to download, no USB drives needed, and no shared computers involved. Every file is encrypted in transit, processed in an isolated cloud environment, and permanently deleted from our servers the moment your print is complete.

Our Team

The MIMO printing kiosk platform was designed and developed by Rathindra to solve real printing problems faced by students on campus daily. Coming from an engineering background, the founder experienced first-hand the frustration of missing assignment deadlines because the campus print shop was closed or the queue was too long.

That personal frustration led to a simple question: why can't printing be as easy as ordering food on Swiggy? The answer became MIMO — a platform that digitises the entire print workflow from your phone to a physical kiosk, with automated payment, file processing, and delivery.

We are a small but dedicated team of engineers and designers committed to improving everyday student life through thoughtful automation. We are constantly improving our systems to provide maximum printer uptime, faster spooling speeds, and bullet-proof file security.

Our Pricing

We keep our pricing transparent and affordable for students:

  • Black & White (Laser): ₹2.30 per page — ideal for assignments, notes, and text documents.
  • Colour (Inkjet): ₹10 per page — ideal for presentations, charts, and photos.
  • No setup fees, no membership required. Pay only for what you print.

Automatic refunds are triggered if a print job fails for any hardware reason, ensuring students are never charged for a failed printout.
