How to Print Directly from Your Mobile Phone: A Complete Guide for College Students

M
MIMO Printing Team
Published on July 7, 2026 • 5 min read

As a student, you've likely found yourself in this situation: you're walking to a lecture hall, your assignment is due in 10 minutes, and you suddenly realize you need a physical printout. In the past, this meant searching for an open computer lab, waiting in a long line, logging in, transferring your files, and digging around for exact change.

MIMO has changed all that by introducing smart, self-service printing kiosks across college campuses. You don't need to carry a laptop or look for cables; you can upload, pay, and print everything directly from your smartphone browser. In this comprehensive guide, we'll walk you through how to utilize your mobile device to print documents, slides, and notes in seconds.

Step 1: Access and Prepare Your Files on Your Phone

Before you head to the kiosk, make sure your document is saved or accessible on your phone. MIMO kiosks support a variety of popular formats, including:

  • PDF (.pdf): The recommended format for assignments, resumes, and text documents. PDFs lock in your formatting, fonts, and layout, ensuring what you see on your screen is exactly what prints out.
  • Word Documents (.doc, .docx): Standard text documents are fully supported.
  • Images (.jpg, .jpeg, .png): Ideal for scanning study notes, receipts, or project sketches.

If your assignment is in Google Drive, OneDrive, or Apple iCloud, open the respective app on your mobile device and export or download the file as a PDF to your local storage. This will make it much easier to select during the upload phase.

Step 2: Upload Your File to printmimo.tech

Once your files are ready, you can upload them from anywhere on campus—whether you're sitting in the library, cafeteria, or walking to class.

  1. Open your mobile web browser (Safari, Chrome, or Firefox) and navigate to printmimo.tech.
  2. Tap the "Start Printing" button to go to the upload page.
  3. Select the files you want to print from your local downloads folder, iCloud files, or file explorer.

Our mobile dashboard is designed to run directly in your browser without requiring you to download any native apps, saving you storage and setup time.

Step 3: Customise Your Print Settings

After selecting your document, the MIMO interface will display the details of your document (such as page count). Now, you can customize your output options based on your assignment requirements:

  • Color vs. Black & White: Black & white laser prints are highly affordable (just ₹2.30 per page), while vibrant color prints are available (₹10 per page) for charts and slides.
  • Copies: Choose how many duplicates of the document you need.
  • Double-sided (Duplex): Check if your document can be printed double-sided to save paper and lighten your bag.

Once you've customized your settings, the dashboard will compute your total fare dynamically. There are no hidden setup fees or surprise charges.

Step 4: Pay Securely via UPI or Card

Tap the payment button. You will be redirected to our secure payment gateway powered by Cashfree. You can complete the payment in seconds using:

  • UPI: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, or your bank's UPI app.
  • Cards: Any debit or credit card.
  • Net Banking: Secure logins for major banks.

After a successful transaction, the browser will instantly display your secure 4-digit print code. This code is also saved in your dashboard and valid for 24 hours.

Step 5: Collect Your Printout at the Kiosk

Walk up to the nearest MIMO kiosk on campus.

  1. Locate the terminal's touch keypad.
  2. Enter your secure 4-digit code.
  3. Confirm your details and watch as the physical printer spools and ejects your pages in real-time.

If there's ever a hardware issue (such as the printer running out of paper or a power fault), the kiosk will report the problem, and our backend will trigger an automatic refund directly to your original payment method. You'll see a green "Refund in Progress" message on your phone screen, ensuring you never pay for a failed print.

Pro-Tip for Fast Printing

Save the printmimo.tech website as a shortcut on your phone's home screen. On Safari (iPhone), tap the Share icon and select "Add to Home Screen". On Chrome (Android), tap the triple dots and select "Add to Home screen". Now you're always one tap away from instant printing.
