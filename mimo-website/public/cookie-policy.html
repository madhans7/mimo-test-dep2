1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, making your experience smoother and more personalized.

2. How MIMO Uses Cookies

MIMO uses a minimal set of cookies to ensure the website and printing service function correctly. We do not use cookies for advertising or third-party tracking purposes.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our payment provider, Cashfree, may place cookies on your device to facilitate secure payment processing. These cookies are governed by Cashfree's own privacy policy.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may impact the functionality of the MIMO service. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies.
  • Block cookies from specific websites.
  • Block all cookies entirely.
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser.
