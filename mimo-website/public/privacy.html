1. Introduction

VisionPrintt Technologies ("MIMO", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the MIMO campus printing service.

2. Information We Collect

  • Documents: Files you upload for printing.
  • Payment Information: Transaction data processed securely via Cashfree. We do not store card details.
  • Usage Data: Basic usage metrics to improve our service.
  • Contact Information: Phone number or name if you reach out to support.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To fulfill your print order.
  • To process payments securely.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To improve our services and user experience.

4. Document Security

All files uploaded to MIMO are encrypted in transit using HTTPS. Documents are permanently and automatically deleted from our servers immediately after printing is complete. We never read, store long-term, or share your documents with any third party.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by law or to process your payment via our secure payment gateway (Cashfree).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us at WhatsApp to exercise these rights.
