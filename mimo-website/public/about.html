Our Mission

MIMO is a smart self-service printing kiosk network designed to make printing easy, fast, cashless, and secure for modern campus students. Created by **VisionPrintt Technologies**, our platform solves the daily frustrations of long lines, exact change requirements, and lack of document privacy at campus print shops.

With MIMO, you simply upload your documents from your phone, customise your print settings, pay securely via UPI/cards, and collect your physical prints from the nearest campus kiosk using a secure 4-digit code in seconds.
